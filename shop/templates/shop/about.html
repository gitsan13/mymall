Project-Mall

Project-Mall is an Indian e-commerce company based in Agra, Jhansi, Varanasi, India. It was founded by Abhay Verma, and Maansi in 2020. The company initially focused on Web development Project sales, before expanding into other Project categories such as App development, Web-development, Mini-Game, Web-App, Templates & many more.
The service runs only in Bundelkhand University, Jhansi. As of July 2020, Project-Mall runs in many cities. This is our starting in e-commerce industry. Project-Mall also owns PhonePe, Google-pay, Paytm, Amazon-Pay a mobile payments service based on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI).
